# Zepto-Inventory-Analysis
## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing inventory performance and sales trends for Zepto, a quick-commerce platform. Using Excel for data preparation and Power BI for visualization, an interactive dashboard was built to monitor stock levels, sales contribution, discounts, and low-stock risks across product categories.

## Objectives
Analyze overall sales and inventory value
Monitor stock availability and low-stock products
Understand discount patterns across categories
Identify high-performing and underperforming categories

## Key KPIs
Total Products
Total Sales
Total Inventory Value
Average Discount Percentage
Low Stock Count
Sales by Category
Available Quantity by Category
Total Products by Category

## Key Insights
Total sales reached ₹12.2B, indicating strong demand across categories.
Inventory value stands at ₹224M, helping assess capital tied in stock.
Average discount of 7.62% shows pricing and promotional strategy impact.
754 products are identified as low stock, highlighting replenishment needs.
Cooking Essentials and Snacks contribute significantly to sales volume.
Certain categories show high discounts but lower sales, indicating optimization opportunities.

## Tools & Technologies
Excel (Data Cleaning & Data Preparation)
Power BI (Interactive Dashboard & Visualization)

## Dashboard Features
Category-based slicers for focused analysis
KPI cards for quick inventory health assessment
Category-wise sales and discount comparison
Stock availability and low-stock identification

## Conclusion
This dashboard provides actionable insights into Zepto’s inventory and sales performance, enabling better stock planning, pricing optimization, and supply chain efficiency through data-driven decisions.
